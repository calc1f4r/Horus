# Manifest and Index Rules

When working with manifests, hunt cards, or `index.json`:

- `DB/index.json` is the Tier 1 router — keep it under 400 lines
- Manifests are auto-generated by `python3 scripts/generate_manifests.py` — do not hand-edit
- Hunt cards in `DB/manifests/huntcards/` are derived from manifests — do not hand-edit
- Each manifest pattern must have `lineStart` and `lineEnd` matching actual .md file content
- `keywords.json` indexes all `codeKeywords` from manifests — regenerated automatically
- `protocolContext` in `index.json` maps protocol types to relevant manifests
- After any DB entry change, always run: `python3 scripts/generate_manifests.py`
